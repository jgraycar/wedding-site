FAQ If you have any questions that aren't addressed here, please feel free to reach out to us anytime!
What time should I arrive at the venue? Please arrive no earlier than 5:00pm, but give yourself time to find your seat at the ceremony by 5:30pm. The reception will end at 11:00pm. What is the dress code for the wedding? Suggested attire is Garden Cocktail! Think midi or full length dresses in bright colors, and suits. This article has some great examples of what we're imagining, but please wear whatever you feel comfortable in. Weather permitting, dinner will be held outside until around 9:00pm, so you may wish to bring a light layer (though Winters is generally still warm this time of year). In terms of footwear, please be prepared for a small amount of walking on grass and gravel. Will parking be available at the venue? Yes, Park Winters has a large parking lot that will accommodate all guests. It also includes three Tesla Destination Chargers. Will transportation to & from the venue be provided? At this time we do not plan to provide transportation to or from the venue. If you wish to avoid driving, we suggest pre-booking an Uber/Lyft to pickup from Park Winters at 11:00pm, as it can be difficult to book one from there. Can I bring a +1? Unfortunately, we are only able to accommodate guests named on your invitation. When you RSVP on our website, all guests in your party will be listed. If you believe there may have been a mistake, please reach out to us!
